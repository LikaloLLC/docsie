What are Enterprise Portals?

Enterprise Portals are secure, multi-tenant documentation platforms that allow you to create separate, branded documentation environments for different customer groups, partners, or business units. Manage everything centrally while providing customized experiences.

  • Secure, dedicated environment for each client
  • Advanced user management and role-based permissions
  • Custom branding for each portal
  • High-performance document delivery

Key Benefits

Scale Effortlessly

Support hundreds or thousands of users across multiple organizations without complexity

Centralized Management

Manage all portals from a single dashboard while maintaining separation

White-Label Ready

Each portal can have its own branding, domain, and customization
